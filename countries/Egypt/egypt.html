Cultural and Historical Tourism

Egypt’s ancient sites are among the world’s most iconic attractions.

The Great Pyramids Of Giza
The Giza Pyramids are a major tourist attraction, offering various ways for visitors to explore the site, including guided tours, camel rides, and opportunities to enter some of the pyramids.

The Grand Egyptian Museum
It offers a unique opportunity to explore the history of one of the world's oldest civilizations in a state-of-the-art facility, making it a must-visit destination for anyone interested in ancient Egypt.

Luxor Temple
Easily accessible for tourists and is illuminated at night, creating a stunning visual experience. Visitors can explore the temple complex, admire its architecture, and view the intricate carvings and hieroglyphics that tell the stories of ancient Egypt.

Beach Tourism

Beach tourism in Egypt is a sun-soaked paradise that beckons travelers with its stunning coastline along the Red Sea and Mediterranean

Hurghada
Beach tourism in Hurghada, Egypt, is a vibrant getaway known for its stunning coastline and world-class coral reefs, attracting snorkelers and divers with rich marine life. With sun-drenched beaches and lively resorts, it offers the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure along the Red Sea.

Sharm EL-Sheikh
Beach tourism in Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt, is celebrated for its stunning beaches and vibrant coral reefs, making it a top destination for snorkeling and diving. With luxurious resorts and lively nightlife, it offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure along the Red Sea.

Alexandreia
Beach tourism in Alexandria, Egypt, offers a unique blend of beautiful Mediterranean beaches and rich historical landmarks, making it a captivating destination. Visitors can enjoy sun-soaked relaxation alongside cultural exploration, with attractions like the Citadel of Qaitbay enhancing the seaside experience.

Medical Tourism

Attracts visitors seeking high-quality medical treatments at affordable prices, including cosmetic surgery, dental care, and fertility treatments, often combined with leisure travel.

Safaga
A coastal town in Egypt, is known for its therapeutic resorts and wellness centers that leverage the area's natural resources for medical and therapeutic treatments. The region is particularly famous for its therapeutic benefits derived from the Red Sea's mineral-rich waters and mud, which are often used in various therapies.

Siwa Oasis
Located in Egypt's Western Desert, is renowned for its unique natural landscape and therapeutic treatments, particularly those utilizing the area's natural mineral springs and mud. The oasis has gained popularity for its wellness and medical therapy options, attracting visitors seeking relaxation and healing.

Authentic Cuisines

Egyptian cuisine is a symphony of flavors, textures, and aromas, reflecting the country's rich diversity and enduring traditions.

Mahshi
Vegetables like zucchini,vine leaves, peppers, or eggplants stuffed with rice, herbs, and minced meat, then simmered in tomato sauce.

Koshari
A street food favorite, Koshari is a mixture of rice, lentils, pasta, and fried onions, topped with tomato sauce and a spicy vinegar dressing.

Molokhia
A leafy green vegetable (jute leaves) cooked in a garlic and coriander broth, usually served with rice or bread and chicken or rabbit.
